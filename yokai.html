Yokai Paradox

Role: Usability and Playtest Lead

Yokai Paradox is a double-sided board game that combines strategy, supernatural themes, and comedic elements, set within a cursed museum. Players take on the roles of three quirky exorcists or the mischievous Yokai, Aiko Nakamura, as they navigate a mysterious museum to either uncover or conceal paranormal secrets.

Game Overview

  • Objective: The exorcists aim to discover the Yokai's hidden location twice to win, while the Yokai attempts to corrupt rooms undetected by staying hidden for multiple turns.
  • Mechanics: Movement is determined by dice rolls, with players engaging in fun challenges (e.g., acting out words, Two Truths and a Lie) to unlock rooms on the board. The dual-sided game board features a day side and a night side, creating dynamic gameplay with hidden information.
  • Components: Includes player role cards, charge orbs, action and word bank cards, and custom exorcist and Yokai abilities to increase complexity and engagement.​

My Role: Usability and Playtest Lead: As the Usability and Playtest Lead, my focus was to ensure that the game mechanics were intuitive and accessible while keeping the experience engaging and fun. I facilitated playtesting sessions to gather feedback on gameplay flow, rules clarity, and overall player experience. Key responsibilities included:

  • Designing Playtests: Organized multiple rounds of playtesting with various player demographics to identify pain points and areas for improvement.
  • Analyzing Player Feedback: Collected data on player behavior, enjoyment, and strategic understanding to refine mechanics and instructions for better clarity and balance.
  • Enhancing Usability: Improved the layout of the board, clarified the rulebook, and optimized the interaction between exorcist abilities and Yokai strategies to ensure smooth and balanced gameplay.​
  • Iterative Design: Worked closely with the development team to implement feedback and adjust game features, from balancing roles to refining challenge cards for better player engagement​

Through this project, I demonstrated strong leadership in usability testing and game refinement, ensuring that Yokai Paradox delivered a compelling and enjoyable experience for all players.
